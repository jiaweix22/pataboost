Platform Transparency and Accountability Act (PATA)

The PATA is a new bipartisan bill that requires tech companies to provide previously undisclosed data to researchers and the public. This new bill was announced in December 2021 and has not been formally introduced.

Construct an effective data exposure and sharing mechanism

To increase the platform transparency of tech companies under PATA

Enable previously blocked independent research initiatives

To enable researchers to access needed data and peer into social problems caused or strengthened by social media

Oversee unchecked market power of tech companies

To guarantee the platform accountability through introducing PATA and further laws and policies

“That equilibrium – where firm insiders know everything and the rest of us are left to guess – is unsustainable. Facebook and the other Silicon Valley Platforms have lost their right to secrecy.”



Nathaniel Persily
